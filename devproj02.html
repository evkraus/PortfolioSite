Goal

Create a fully responsive website, this must have a minimum of 4 pages that have 1 section each or 1 page with 4 different major sections. This websites topic was completely up to us. There was also a requirement to use at least one JS plugin.

Process

The beginning of this project we were tasked with creating an initial proposal that had a brief description of the website’s topic, a sitemap, and a low fidelity mockup of at least one page. My original topic with the proposal was a websit that sells and distributes video games online like steam but for more than just PC. I called this site “GameLink”, below are the sitemap and mockup:

I liked this topic, however as I revised my mockups with pears, my idea was bigger than both my skills and the deadline. I ended up altering the topic to be photography for my father. I chose this topic as I had a JS plugin in mind already (nanogallery02) and as it was someone, I knew I could easily acquire the photos needed.

I began by collecting numerous pictures from my father of a variety of things such as plants, animals, people, and scenery. Once I got the photos, I created my files ahead of time to keep them clean:

I then began by coding the gallery page first as it would be the page using the JS plugin NanoGallery02 and take the longest. I first got all the pictures onto the page and then began styling and detailing things such as borders and titles for the pictures with the plugin making it look as modern as I could.
